Help get open hardware and open design on the SXSW agenda

christianvillum
Aug 23, 2018

With OSHWA’s Michael Weinberg I’ve submitted a proposal to the SXSW conference in Austin Texas to host a session/meetup on open design as component in the business models to drive manufacturing and hardware in the future.

We could use your help: The more votes and social media sharings that our proposal gets, the more likely it is to make to the final program.

To cast your vote, please go to https://panelpicker.sxsw.com/vote/89927, register (it’s quick) and press ‘upvote’. Also, the social media buttons on the side give juice to the score as well, so feel free to use those to share also if you like.

Thanks everyone!
