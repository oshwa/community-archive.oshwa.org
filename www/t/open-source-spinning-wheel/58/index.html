Open Source Spinning Wheel

kellbot
Feb 15, 2017

I thought this was a neat project, and they just finished up a kickstarter for the latest iteration. It’s on open source electric spinning wheel, for spinning fiber into yarn.

Electric Eel Wheel

banner.jpg800x600 68.4 KB

I’ve seen similar wheels at fiber festivals, and while they’re beautifully made they’re a little out of the price range for many hobbiests (over $1000) so it’s really need to see something like this for those of us who don’t mind tinkering with the machine a bit.
