Please list any equipment, instrumentation, or devices that you think should be available for less via an open-source, possibly DIY, approach for your scientific work here.

Some that are already being worked on:

  • Animal tracking tags
  • Materials characterization jig (compare to Instron load frame)
  • Cosmic ray detector
  • Atomic Force Microscope (AFM)
  • Weather radar
  • Frequency counter
  • Multimeter
  • Precision temperature controller
  • Precision environmental chamber (T/RH/P control)
  • Sound Level Meter (SLM)
  • Sample/mix shaker
  • UV meter
  • Spectrophotometer
  • Magnetometric imager
  • IMU logger (dead reckoning tracker)
